# **App Name**: FirstRead

## Core Features:

- Large Letter Display: Displays a single, prominent lowercase letter on the main screen, making it easy for the child to focus.
- Interactive Letter Cycling: Advances to the next letter in the selected sequence with a click on the page or by pressing the spacebar, providing simple and clear interaction.
- Letter Customization Menu: A user-friendly popover menu allows parents to easily select or deselect specific letters that will be included in the practice set.
- Persistent Selections: Saves the parent's chosen letter selections to ensure they are remembered across different sessions.
- Encouragement Generator: An AI tool that occasionally generates simple, positive messages to offer encouragement to the child during their learning session.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A warm, playful orange (#F9991F) to evoke energy and positivity for learning.
- Background color: A soft, warm beige (#FAF5ED) provides a calming canvas that allows the displayed letter to stand out.
- Accent color: A cheerful, muted pinkish-red (#DD6363) is used for interactive elements, creating visual interest and guidance.
- All text and display letters use 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif font known for its warmth and excellent legibility, making it child-friendly and clear.
- Simple, clean outline icons are used for interface elements like the menu toggle, ensuring they are easy to understand without being distracting.
- The primary focus is a large, centrally aligned letter, ensuring maximum visibility. The letter selection menu appears as a subtle popover, minimizing visual clutter during practice.
- Subtle and smooth transitions, such as a gentle fade or brief slide, are used when changing letters to maintain focus without being overly flashy.
